Hello everyone!!! Myself is Dr. Pangambam Sendash Singh (Ph.D.)

I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Physics (Hons.) with Mathematics and Computer Science from D.M. College of Science, Imphal (under Manipur University) in the year 2012, thereafter Master's degree in Computer Science from Banaras Hindu University (BHU), Varanasi in the year 2015. I have obtained my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Institute of Science, Banaras Hindu University (BHU) in the year 2022. I passed the UGC - National Eligibility Test (NET) in Computer Science and Applications during my Master's studies and I was awarded the UGC's Junior Research Fellowship (JRF) in December 2015.

My Ph.D. research work was supervised by Prof. S. Karthikeyan, former Head, Department of Computer Science, Banaras Hindu University (BHU). My research area includes Computational Intelligence, Deep Learning and its applications in Multispectral and Hyperspectral Image Analysis.

I take a lot of interest in writing poems, listening music, singing and playing musical instruments.

Prior to obtaining my Ph.D. degree, I have been selected for the position of Postdoctoral Researcher on Machine Learning based Detection of Archaeological Sites from Multispectral Satellite Images at the Institut Català d'Arqueologia Clàssica (ICAC), Spain and I am going to join the position very soon.
